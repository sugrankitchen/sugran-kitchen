Our Story
आमची कहाणी

Sugran Kitchen was born from a simple, beautiful belief — that the best food is made at home, with love, using pure ingredients and recipes passed down through generations.

Started by Swati Muley in Arvi, Maharashtra, Sugran Kitchen brings the warmth of a mother's kitchen right to your doorstep. Every dish we prepare carries the essence of Maharashtrian tradition — the flavours of home that you grew up loving.

आम्ही प्रत्येक पदार्थ मनापासून बनवतो — जसे घरचे जेवण असते. ताजे साहित्य, शुद्ध पाककला, आणि प्रत्येक घासात प्रेम!
